# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want `blah-code` to be an open, respectful, harassment-free project for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent
- accept feedback and corrections gracefully
- prioritize collaboration over ego

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or personal attacks
- doxxing or sharing private info without permission
- abusive language or intimidation
- repeated bad-faith disruption

## Scope

This applies in repository spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, commits) and in project-linked community spaces.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to `blah.chat@bhekani.com`.

Maintainers may remove content, lock threads, reject contributions, or ban participants depending on severity/repeat behavior.

## Enforcement ladder

1. correction
2. warning
3. temporary ban
4. permanent ban
